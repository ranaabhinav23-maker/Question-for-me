Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built specifically for medical students who want to study with purpose, not just put in hours.

🤖
AI MCQ Generator
Generate unlimited, clinically-accurate multiple choice questions from your own notes. Filter by block, difficulty, and topic. Never run out of practice material.
📖
Lecture Tracker
Track every lecture across every block. Mark first and second passes, see exactly how many hours of content remain, and never lose your place again.
🌿
Focus Timer
Built-in Pomodoro-style focus sessions with customisable work and break intervals. Track your total study hours with automatic session logging.
🃏
Smart Flashcards
Create and review flashcards linked to your notes. Spaced repetition built in. Review weak areas automatically identified by your MCQ performance.
📊
Analytics Dashboard
See your progress at a glance. Activity heatmaps, block-by-block breakdowns, MCQ accuracy trends, and weekly study hour summaries.
🧠
AI Explanations
Get instant AI-powered explanations for any question you get wrong. Understand the pathophysiology, not just the answer. Learn from every mistake.
